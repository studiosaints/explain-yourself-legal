FAQ

  • Mic not working? iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → Explain Yourself → On.
  • No audio? Check volume and the Silent switch.
  • Restore purchases/packs: In the app go to Store → Restore Purchases.
  • Voice vs typing: You can answer by voice or text on your turn.

Troubleshooting

  1. Force-quit and relaunch the app.
  2. Reboot iPhone.
  3. Reinstall if needed (purchases can be restored).
