Quick FAQ

  • Mic not working? iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → Explain Yourself → On.
  • No audio? Turn up volume and check the Silent switch.
  • Restore purchases/packs: In the app go to Store → Restore Purchases.
  • Voice vs typing: On your turn you can answer by voice or text.
  • Age rating: The game is intended for users 13+.

Troubleshooting

  1. Force-quit and relaunch the app.
  2. Reboot your iPhone.
  3. Reinstall if issues persist (purchases can be restored).
