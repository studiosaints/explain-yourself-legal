Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 3, 2025

App: Explain Yourself

Developer: Studio Saints

The Simple Version: We barely collect anything. Your fun stays private, your data stays yours, and we're not sneaky about it.

What We Actually Do (Spoiler: Very Little)

What We Collect:

  • User ID: A random identifier from Apple/Google when you sign in (so we can save your purchases)
  • Purchase History: What packs you've bought (so we can restore them)
  • Voice Recordings: Temporarily processed for AI responses, then immediately deleted
  • Game Data: Your game history and player stats (stored only on your device)

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your real name
  • Your contacts
  • Your location (beyond country for legal compliance)
  • Your photos
  • Anything from your microphone except during gameplay

Where Your Data Lives

  • On your device: Game history, player profiles, photos you take
  • Apple/Google: Your purchase receipts (we don't control this)
  • Our servers: Just your user ID and purchase info

How We Use Your Info

  • User ID: To save and restore your purchases
  • Voice recordings: To generate AI responses (then deleted immediately)
  • Purchase data: To unlock the content you paid for
  • Country detection: To show GDPR consent for EU users

Your Rights (You're In Control)

  • Delete your account: Settings → Delete Account (removes everything)
  • Clear your data: Settings → Clear Game History / Clear Leaderboard
  • Control purchases: Managed through Apple/Google, not us

Who We Share With

Short answer: Basically nobody.

  • AI services: Voice recordings for processing (immediately deleted)
  • Apple/Google: Purchase verification only
  • Nobody else: We don't sell, rent, or share your data

Security

We use industry-standard security. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. But let's be honest - since we barely collect anything, there's not much to worry about.

Children's Privacy

This app isn't specifically for kids, but we don't collect personal information from anyone under 13 without parental consent. Actually, we don't collect much personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the date above and let you know in the app. We're not planning any major changes since our approach is "collect as little as possible."
