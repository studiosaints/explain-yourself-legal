Privacy Policy

Who We Are

Studio Saints created "Explain Yourself" as a fun party game. We believe in keeping things simple and respecting your privacy.

Contact Information:

  • App Developer: Studio Saints
  • Email: hello@studiosaints.io

How The Data Gets Used:

When You Sign In

  • We get a random user ID from Apple or Google (like "user123")
  • This lets you keep your purchases if you reinstall the app
  • We never see your email, real name, or personal info

When You Play A Round

  • Player names you type stay on YOUR phone only
  • Voice recordings go to OpenAI to turn speech into text, then get deleted forever
  • Your answers and game history stay on YOUR phone only
  • Your photos/selfies never leave your device and are used for a more fun gameplay only
  • Win/loss stats stay on YOUR phone for the leaderboard

When You Make A Purchase

  • Apple handles all payments (we never see your credit card)
  • We track what packs you bought (through the user ID) so you keep them forever

What We Don't Do

  • No GPS or location tracking
  • No reading your contacts or photos
  • No selling your data to advertisers
  • No analytics or spy stuff
  • No storing your voice recordings
  • No accessing your real name or email

Region Detection (For EU Privacy Laws)

  • We check your phone's region setting (like "United States" or "Germany")
  • This is the same setting the App Store uses
  • We only use this to show GDPR consent for EU users during sign in

Where Your Data Lives

  • User ID: With Firebase (Google's secure service)
  • Voice processing, player names, and your answers: OpenAI (very briefly for processing, then deleted)
  • Player selfies: On your phone ONLY
  • Purchases: With Apple (their secure system)

The Legal Stuff

We follow GDPR (European privacy laws) and COPPA (kid protection laws). If something changes, we'll let you know. This app is for ages 13+ because the AI may at times be unpredictable. And because what you input is what it outputs in terms of content.
